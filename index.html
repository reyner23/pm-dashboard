Connect your Google Sheet

Follow these steps to connect a Google Sheet that your whole team can edit. The dashboard will pull live data every time you refresh.

  1. Create a Google Sheet and add these columns in row 1: Name, Team, Owner, Status, Priority, Quarter, Due Date, Figma Link, Notes
  2. Go to File → Share → Publish to web
  3. Choose Sheet1, format CSV, and click Publish
  4. Copy the published URL and paste it below
